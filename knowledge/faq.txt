Zyraluxe — Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do you ship internationally?
A: No, we currently deliver only within India. Free shipping is available on orders above Rs.799.

Q: How much is shipping?
A: Shipping is FREE on all orders above Rs.799. A standard shipping fee applies for orders below Rs.799.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept multiple UPI IDs at checkout. (Cash on delivery is not mentioned on our site.)

Q: What kind of jewellery do you sell?
A: We sell affordable fashion / costume jewellery for women and girls — earrings, jhumkas, necklace sets, chokers, pendants, bangles, bracelets, anklets, and combos. Materials include alloy gold-plated, oxidised / german silver, rhodium-plated, pearl, kundan, beads, and stone work. We are not solid gold or certified diamond jewellery.

Q: What is your price range?
A: Most items are between Rs.40 and Rs.350, and many are under Rs.200, so we are a budget-friendly brand.

Q: How do I track my order?
A: Go to https://zyraluxe.in/track-order/ and enter your Order ID and billing email (both are in your confirmation email).

Q: What is your return policy?
A: You can request a return within 2 HOURS of delivery, if the item is unused and in original packaging. Refunds are processed in 7-10 business days. For damaged items, report within 2 hours with photos. Full policy: https://zyraluxe.in/return-policy/

Q: Can I exchange for a different size or product?
A: Exchanges are only for damaged or defective items. For a different size/product, place a new order after your return is approved.

Q: How do I contact you?
A: Email zyraluxeofficial@gmail.com. Support is available 24x7. You can also reach us on Instagram @zyraluxeoffical.

Q: Are your products real gold or diamond?
A: No. Zyraluxe sells fashion / imitation jewellery (alloy, oxidised silver, rhodium-plated, pearl, american/austrian diamond accents, kundan). They are stylish, lightweight, and affordable, not solid precious metal.
