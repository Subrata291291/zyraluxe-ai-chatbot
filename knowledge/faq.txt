Zyraluxe — Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do you ship internationally?
A: Yes. We ship across India and to most international destinations. Domestic orders usually arrive in 3-5 business days. International delivery takes 7-14 business days depending on the destination and customs clearance.

Q: How much does shipping cost?
A: Shipping is FREE on all orders above Rs.999 within India. Orders below Rs.999 carry a flat Rs.49 shipping fee. International shipping is calculated at checkout based on weight and destination.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept UPI, all major debit and credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay, American Express), Net Banking, and Cash on Delivery (COD) for domestic orders up to Rs.5000.

Q: Are your jewellery pieces certified?
A: All gold and diamond jewellery from Zyraluxe comes with a hallmark (BIS) certification. Diamond pieces above 0.3 carat include an IGI or GIA grading certificate.

Q: Can I customize or engrave jewellery?
A: Yes, we offer engraving on most rings, pendants, and bracelets for a nominal fee of Rs.199. Customization requests can be placed through our support team.

Q: Do you offer gift wrapping?
A: Every Zyraluxe order ships in a premium reusable jewellery box at no extra cost. Paid gift wrapping with a handwritten note is available at checkout for Rs.99.

Q: How can I track my order?
A: Once your order ships, you receive an SMS and email with a tracking link. You can also track from the "My Orders" section of your account.

Q: What if an item is out of stock?
A: Out-of-stock items show an option to "Notify Me". We restock best-sellers frequently, usually within 2-3 weeks.
