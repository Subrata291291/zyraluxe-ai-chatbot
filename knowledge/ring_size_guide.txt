Zyraluxe — Ring Size Guide

How to measure at home:
1. Take a strip of paper and wrap it around the base of your finger.
2. Mark where it overlaps and measure the length in millimetres.
3. Match the measurement to the chart below.

Circumference to Indian size (approx):
- 50 mm = size 10
- 52 mm = size 12
- 54 mm = size 14
- 56 mm = size 16
- 58 mm = size 18
- 60 mm = size 20
- 62 mm = size 22
- 64 mm = size 24

Tips:
- Measure at the end of the day when fingers are warmest.
- The ring should slide on easily but resist slightly at the knuckle.
- Wide-band rings fit tighter; choose half a size up.
- For surprise gifting, borrow a ring they already wear and measure its inner diameter.

Resizing: We offer one free ring resize within 30 days of purchase (applicable to most designs; some open-ended and full-eternity styles cannot be resized).

If between sizes: always size up for comfort.
