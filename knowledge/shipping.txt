Zyraluxe - Shipping & Delivery

Free shipping: We offer FREE delivery on all orders above Rs.799 (Rs.799 and above). For orders below Rs.799, a standard shipping fee applies.

Delivery areas: We deliver across India only. International shipping is not offered.

Delivery time: Orders are usually dispatched and delivered within a few business days. Exact timelines are shared after you place the order in your confirmation email.

Tracking: Track your order from the Track Order page (https://zyraluxe.in/track-order/) by entering your Order ID and billing email. Both are in your order confirmation email.

Order issues: If you do not receive a confirmation email or tracking details, contact us at zyraluxeofficial@gmail.com.
