Zyraluxe — Shipping Policy

Coverage: We deliver across all 28 states and 8 union territories of India. International shipping is available to the USA, UK, UAE, Canada, Australia, and Singapore.

Domestic delivery times:
- Metro cities: 2-3 business days
- Tier 2/3 cities: 3-5 business days
- Remote areas: 5-7 business days

International delivery times: 7-14 business days after dispatch, subject to customs clearance at the destination country.

Shipping charges (India):
- Orders Rs.999 and above: FREE standard shipping
- Orders below Rs.999: flat Rs.49
- Express delivery (metro only): Rs.99, delivered in 24-48 hours

International shipping: Calculated at checkout based on parcel weight and destination zone. Duties and taxes, if applicable, are borne by the customer as per the destination country's import rules.

Order processing: All orders are processed within 24 hours on business days. Made-to-order and engraved pieces may take an additional 2-4 business days.

Tracking: A tracking ID is shared via SMS and email once the courier picks up the parcel.

Address changes: Address can be updated only if the order has not been dispatched. Contact support immediately for changes.

Lost or delayed parcels: If a parcel is undelivered beyond the estimated window, our team raises a courier investigation. A replacement or refund is issued within 7 business days of confirmation.
