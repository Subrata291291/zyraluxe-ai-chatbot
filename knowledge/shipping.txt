Zyraluxe — Shipping & Delivery

Free shipping: We offer FREE delivery on all orders above Rs.799. For orders below Rs.799, a standard shipping fee applies.

Delivery areas: We deliver across India. (International shipping is not offered.)

Delivery time: Orders are usually dispatched and delivered within a few business days. Exact timelines are shared after you place the order and receive the confirmation email.

Tracking: You can track your order from the Track Order page (https://zyraluxe.in/track-order/) by entering your Order ID and billing email. The Order ID is given on your receipt and in the confirmation email you receive after ordering.

Order issues: If you do not receive a confirmation email or tracking details, contact us at zyraluxeofficial@gmail.com.
