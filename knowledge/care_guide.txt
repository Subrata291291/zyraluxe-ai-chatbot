Zyraluxe — Jewellery Care Guide (fashion / costume jewellery)

Our pieces are alloy, oxidised/german silver, rhodium-plated, and stone/pearl work. With a little care they stay beautiful longer.

Daily wear:
- Put jewellery on after perfume, hairspray, and lotion have dried.
- Remove before washing hands, swimming, bathing, or heavy sweating.
- Avoid contact with water, sweat, and chemicals — this prevents tarnish and plating loss.

Cleaning:
- Wipe gently with a soft, dry cotton cloth after wearing.
- Do not use water, soap, or alcohol-based cleaners on oxidised or plated pieces.
- For pearls, wipe with a slightly damp soft cloth and dry immediately.

Storage:
- Store each piece separately in a zip-lock or air-tight pouch to reduce exposure to air and moisture.
- Keep away from direct sunlight and humidity.
- Add a small silica gel packet to control moisture.

Tarnish: Oxidised and plated jewellery may naturally dull over time. Keep it dry and stored air-tight to slow this down.
