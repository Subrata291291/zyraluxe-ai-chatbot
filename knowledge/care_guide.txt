Zyraluxe — Jewellery Care Guide

Daily habits:
- Put jewellery on last, after perfume, hairspray, and lotion.
- Take off rings and bracelets before washing hands, swimming, or gym workouts.
- Remove pieces before sleeping to avoid tangling and bending.

Cleaning:
- Gold and diamond: warm water + few drops of mild dish soap, soft brush, rinse, dry with a lint-free cloth.
- Silver: use a silver polishing cloth; store with anti-tarnish strips.
- Pearls: wipe with a damp soft cloth after wearing; never soak.
- Avoid ultrasonic cleaners for delicate, fracture-filled, or pearl pieces.

Storage:
- Store each piece separately in a soft pouch or lined box.
- Keep in a cool, dry place away from sunlight.
- Add a silica gel packet to control moisture.

Plating care:
- Gold-plated and rose-gold pieces last longer when kept dry.
- Re-plating is available through our service centre.

Professional check-up:
- Have clasps, prongs, and stone settings checked once a year.
- Tighten any loose stones promptly to prevent loss.

Warranty: Our 1-year manufacturing warranty covers plating, clasps, and settings against defects (not loss or accidental damage).
