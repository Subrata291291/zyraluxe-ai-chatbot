Zyraluxe — About Us

Zyraluxe is a modern Indian jewellery house that blends traditional craftsmanship with contemporary design. We started with a simple belief: fine jewellery should be beautiful, certified, and honestly priced.

Our craft: Each piece is designed in-house and crafted by skilled artisans using 14k and 18k gold, 925 sterling silver, and conflict-free natural and lab-grown diamonds. Every gold and diamond item carries BIS hallmarking and recognized grading certificates.

Our promise:
- Certified metals and stones
- Transparent, markup-free pricing
- Secure, insured shipping
- 30-day easy returns
- 1-year manufacturing warranty

Collections: We offer necklaces, earrings, rings, bangles, bracelets, pendants, jhumkas, and bridal sets for everyday wear, gifting, and weddings.

Sustainability: We use recycled gold where possible and conflict-free stones. Packaging is reusable and plastic-light.

Support: Our AI concierge and human support team are available to help you find the right piece, check sizing, or track an order.
