Zyraluxe — Store Overview

Zyraluxe (brand also shown as "Zyra Luxe") is an online fashion jewellery store in India. We sell affordable, trendy costume and imitation jewellery for women and girls — perfect for everyday wear, festivals, weddings, and gifting.

What we sell:
- Earrings (studs, hoops, jhumkas, chandbalis, danglers, oxidised earrings)
- Pendants / Pendents
- Oxidised Jhumka
- Sitahar (traditional necklace)
- Chokker / Choker necklace sets
- Necklace sets (with matching earrings)
- Combo packs (e.g. pack of 4 earrings)
- Bangles
- Bracelets
- Anklets

Materials used: alloy gold-plated, oxidised / german silver, rhodium-plated, pearl, american diamond / austrian diamond accents, kundan, beads, and stones. Our jewellery is fashion / costume jewellery, not solid gold or certified diamonds.

Price range: Most items are priced between Rs.40 and Rs.350. Many items are under Rs.200, making Zyraluxe a budget-friendly fashion jewellery brand.

Free shipping: FREE delivery on all orders above Rs.799.

Payment: We accept multiple UPI IDs at checkout. (No COD mentioned on the site.)

Support: 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Trusted by 1000+ happy customers.
