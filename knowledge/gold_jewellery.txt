Zyraluxe — Gold Jewellery Guide

Purity: Our gold jewellery is available in 14k (58.5% gold) and 18k (75% gold). 18k has a richer colour and higher gold content; 14k is more durable for daily wear.

Hallmarking: Every gold piece is BIS-hallmarked with the purity stamp (e.g., 750 for 18k, 585 for 14k), the Zyraluxe logo, and the assaying centre mark.

Colour options:
- Yellow gold: classic, warm tone
- Rose gold: blended with copper for a pink hue
- White gold: alloyed with palladium/nickel and rhodium-plated for a silver look

Care for gold:
- Store separately in a soft pouch to avoid scratches.
- Clean with a mild soap solution and a soft brush; buff with a lint-free cloth.
- Avoid contact with perfume, chlorine, and cosmetics.
- White gold may need rhodium re-plating every 12-18 months.

Best for gifting: Gold rings, mangalsutras, and lightweight necklaces are popular gifting choices.

Investment note: 18k retains more intrinsic gold value; 14k offers better resistance to dents for active lifestyles.
