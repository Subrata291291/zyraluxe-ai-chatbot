Zyraluxe — Diamond Guide

Natural vs lab-grown: We offer both natural diamonds and lab-grown diamonds. Lab-grown stones have the same physical and optical properties as natural diamonds, are certified, and are typically more affordable.

The 4 Cs:
- Cut: most important for sparkle. We recommend Excellent or Very Good cuts.
- Colour: graded D (colourless) to Z. D-F are colourless; G-J look near-colourless and offer great value.
- Clarity: measures inclusions. VS1-VS2 look clean to the naked eye; SI1 is often a smart value pick.
- Carat: stone weight. A 0.30 carat stone is a popular entry; 0.50+ is considered statement size.

Certification: Diamonds of 0.30 carat and above include an IGI or GIA certificate with the stone's unique grading report number.

Settings: Prong, bezel, halo, and pave settings affect both look and security of the stone.

Care:
- Soak in warm water with mild dish soap, brush gently, rinse, and pat dry.
- Avoid harsh chemicals and ultrasonic cleaners for fracture-filled stones.
- Have prongs checked annually by a jeweller.

Budget tip: Choosing G-H colour and VS clarity can save significantly versus D-F/VVS while looking identical to most eyes.
