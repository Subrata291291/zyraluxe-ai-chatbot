Zyraluxe — Returns, Refunds & Exchange Policy

Return window: We accept returns and exchanges within 30 days of delivery for most products, provided they are unworn, unused, and in original packaging with tags and certificates intact.

Non-returnable items:
- Customized or engraved jewellery
- Earrings and nose pins (hygiene reasons) unless defective
- Items marked "Final Sale"

How to start a return:
1. Go to "My Orders" and select the item.
2. Choose Return or Exchange and a reason.
3. Our team approves within 24-48 hours.
4. A courier picks up the item (free reverse pickup in most Indian pin codes).

Condition check: Returned items are inspected at our quality center. If the item shows signs of wear, damage, or missing certification, the return may be rejected and sent back to you.

Refunds:
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method.
- Card/UPI/Net Banking: 5-7 business days.
- COD orders: refunded to your bank account or UPI (share details in the return form).
- Store credit: instant, if you choose that option.

Exchanges: Size exchanges for rings and bracelets are free once. Replacement for manufacturing defects is always free.

Defective or wrong item: If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect product, report it within 48 hours of delivery with photos. We arrange an immediate free replacement.

Warranty: All Zyraluxe jewellery carries a 1-year manufacturing warranty covering plating, clasps, and stone settings (not loss or accidental damage).
