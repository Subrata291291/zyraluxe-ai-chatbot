Zyraluxe — Return, Refund & Exchange Policy

Return window: You may request a return within 2 HOURS from the time of delivery. Because our return window is very short, please inspect your order as soon as it arrives.

Eligibility: The item must be unused, unworn, and in the same condition you received it. It must be in the original packaging with tags, bills, and accessories included. Customized, personalized items, sale items, and gift cards are non-returnable.

How to start a return: Contact our support team at zyraluxeofficial@gmail.com with your order details. Once your request is approved, we will give you the return shipping address and instructions. Customers pay the return shipping cost unless the item is damaged, defective, or incorrect.

Refunds: After we receive and inspect your return, we will tell you if the refund is approved or rejected. If approved, the refund is processed within 7-10 business days to your original payment method. Shipping fees, if any, are not refunded.

Exchanges: Exchanges are allowed ONLY for damaged or defective items. If you need a different size or product, please place a new order once your return is approved.

Damaged or wrong items: If you receive a damaged, defective, or wrong product, notify us WITHIN 2 HOURS of delivery with photos/videos as proof. We will arrange a replacement or full refund.

Contact for returns: zyraluxeofficial@gmail.com
